About this project:

 I have analyzed the " Store Annual Report" and created a powerful dashboard using Excel to gain insights that can assist store

Objective :The primary objective of “ Store Annual Report 2022” was to analyze customer behavior and sales performance and grow more in upcoming years.

Steps followed:

1.Data Extraction-Data was extracted in form of Excel(.XLSX) sheet From Kaggle.

2.Data cleaning- This will ensure accuracy and reliability of raw data. Inconsistent values and NULL entries were fixed.

2.Data Processing: Two columns ‘Age Group’ and ‘Month’ were added for deeper analysis of dataset.

4.Data analysis: Pivot Tables were created for uncovering patterns and trends.

5.Data Visualization: Various Charts, graphs and slicers were created for visualizing the key insights.
